Rules			
Draft pick selection order will be randomly selected immediatley before drafting.			
			
Roster(8):			
Each team will draft:			
1 QB			
1 RB			
1 WR			
1 TE			
1 FLEX (RB,WR,TE)			
1 Game Spread			
1 Game Over/Under			
1 FLEX (Either Spread or Over/Under)			
			
Scoring:			
QB: 1 pt 25 Passing Yards, 4 pts Passing TD, 6 pts Rushing TD, 1 pt 20 Rushing Yards, -2 pts Turnover			
RB: 1 pt 10 Yards, 6 pts TD, -2 Turnover	.5 Points PPR		
WR: 1 pt 10 Yards, 6 pts TD, -2 Turnover	.5 Points PPR		
TE: 1 pt 10 Yards, 6 pts TD, -2 Turnover	.5 Points PPR		
Spread: 10 Points If Bet is Won (Bonus: for each additional covering point up to 10 max)			
Example: Green Bay -4. Actual score is Green Bay 32, Jacksonville 20. 18 Points Awarded.			
Over/Under: 10 Points If Bet is Won (Bonus: for each additional covering point for over and 2 for each covering point for under, up to 10 max)			
Example: Green Bay/Jacksonville Over 49. Actual score is Green Bay 32, Jacksonville 20. 13 Points Awarded.			
			
A tease bonus is awarded for the round that Over/Under or Spread is drafted in according to the following chart:			
Over/Under		Spread	
Round 1 = 7 pt tease		Round 1 = 3.5 pt tease	
Round 2 = 6 pt tease		Round 2 = 3 pt tease	
Round 3 = 5 pt tease		Round 3 = 2.5 pt tease	
Round 4 = 4 pt tease		Round 4 = 2 pt tease	
Round 5 = 3 pt tease		Round 5 = 1.5 pt tease	
Round 6 = 2 pt tease		Round 6 = 1 pt tease	
Round 7 = 1 pt tease		Round 7 = .5 pt tease	
			
A player cannot intentionally draft both sides of a Spread or Over/Under			
A push results in no points awarded			
If a drafted player is not active, manager is allowed to drop him and pick up a replacement prior to kickoff.
